What is OpenGrad?

Multi-year programs of study modeled on doctoral programs at top institutions.

  • Graduate-level — Equivalent in depth and rigor to a doctoral program
  • AI-facilitated — Curriculum, evaluation, and discussion powered by AI
  • Non-credentialed — No degree, only genuine formation
  • Primary sources — You read alone, AI operates at program level
