## Program Structure

- **Duration:** 4 years part-time (10-15 hours/week)
- **Per semester:** 16 weeks, 4 units
- **Writing:** Weekly responses (500 words), unit essays (2,000 words), semester synthesis (5,000 words)
- **Final project:** Book-length work (Year 4)
